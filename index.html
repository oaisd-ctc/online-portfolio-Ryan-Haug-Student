About Me

My journey:
Ever since I was a kid, the idea of creating through technology has always piqued my curiosity. Whether it was building machines with Minecraft Redstone or experimenting in Scratch, I loved finding logical solutions through play. That passion led me to build my first computer at 13, though I only dabbled in programming until high school. Things really took off in my junior year when I joined the OAISD Careerline Tech Center for software and game development. There, I began with basic HTML and CSS, quickly finishing labs and earning certifications. We progressed to C# console applications in .NET, and finally to Unity for game development; where the combination of physics, logic, and creativity hooked me. I’m returning for my senior year to dive deeper into JavaScript, SQL, and more advanced concepts.

My Personal Life:
Outside of programming and school, I’m someone who loves staying active and creative in other ways too. I ride a motorcycle because I love the feeling of freedom and the open road. I’m also into photography, I don’t have a professional setup, but I enjoy capturing moments when I can. When I’m not out and about, I like spending time with friends, either at the beach or gaming together. Some of my favorite games are Valorant, Assetto Corsa, Risk of Rain 2, and of course, Minecraft one of the games that helped spark this whole journey in the first place.

3 Day Project | WPF app

This was one of our first big c# projects in the Tech Center. The goal was to collaborate with 2 other people and build a .Net application using tools like github projects and branching, all while ensuring there were no merge conflicts and bugs. We had 3 days to complete the whole project including documentation, and my group decided instead of a .Net application we were going to use Windows Presentation Format (WPF) to create a pacman clone instead. My role in this project was to handle user input and some visual aspects such as the walls.

Final Drive | Unity XR Game

As the final project for our class I wanted to challenge myself to create a cool project, I thought what better way than to make a VR game. So Final Drive was created, the goal of this game is to just make it through traffic as long as possible acheiving a high score. Imagine this as like a endless runner, but with more freedom on choice with how you move.

Reborn By the Knight | Unity 2d Game

As my first full Project in unity I wanted to create something simple yet replayable. The aim of the game is simple, Just stay alive as long as possible while progressively you and the enemys get stronger.
